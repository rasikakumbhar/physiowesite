About Physiotherapy

At PhysioPoint Physiotherapy, we believe that healing is not just about treatment — it's about personalized care, trust, and long-term well-being. Our modern clinic in Ambernath West is designed to create a calming, welcoming space where patients feel supported at every step.

With a focus on evidence-based practice, we offer services ranging from orthopedic recovery, neuro-rehabilitation, pediatric therapy, women’s health, and sports injury management. Every treatment plan is tailored to your unique goals, whether it's restoring strength, improving mobility, or enhancing athletic performance.

We are proud to serve our community with compassion, professionalism, and advanced therapeutic techniques to ensure lasting results.
